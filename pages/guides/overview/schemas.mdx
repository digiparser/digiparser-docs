---
title: Understanding Schemas
---

A schema describes the structure and organization of data, and it blueprints how information is stored, processed, and interpreted. 
Schemas establish the anticipated data format and relationships, assuring consistency and accuracy. 

DigiParser enables customers to create custom extraction schemas, adapting the data extraction process to unique requirements. 
This versatility ensures the collected data perfectly aligns with business objectives, smoothly connecting with over 5,000 products, including major platforms like QuickBooks, Xero, and Salesforce.
